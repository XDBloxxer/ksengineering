About Me

I'm a multi-disciplinary engineer with experience spanning mechanical design, structural analysis, electrical systems, and aerospace applications. I thrive at the intersection of these fields, where the most interesting problems live.

My work is defined by a commitment to rigorous first-principles thinking — understanding why things work, not just how to make them work. Whether designing a load-bearing structure or laying out a PCB, the same systematic approach applies.

I also write software when the problem calls for it — primarily for simulation, automation, and data analysis tooling — but engineering hardware is where I'm most at home.
